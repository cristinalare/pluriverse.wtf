WTF is the
Pluriverse Guide?

An invitation to dream wildy together.

  • a schelling point for the creation of Pluriversal culture.
  • an invitation to exchange stories, ideas, and designs.
  • a challenge to the vision of Stephenson's metaverse.
  • a challenge to the vision of Zuck's metaverse.
  • a challenge to build a bottoms-up vision of many metaverses.
  • an place to intentionally-future a p2p metaverse, bottoms up, by & for the pluriversal people.
  • a place to discuss public goods, Moloch, solarpunk, coordination, cypherpunk, terrapunk, lunarpunk, regenpunk, & a plurality of many value systems.
  • a place to fund, create, & distribute pluriversal media.
